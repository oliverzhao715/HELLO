# 🐉 D&D Endless Boss Rush: The Ultimate Keyboard-Smashing Campaign

Welcome, brave Wordsmith, to the only dungeon crawl where your most powerful weapon isn't a +3 Holy Avenger—it's your mechanical keyboard with RGB lighting. 

This is a standalone, web-based typing gauntlet where legendary monsters take turns trying to eat your face, and the only way to stop them is by typing magical incantations faster than your hands can panic.

## 🕹️ Live Demo
Deploy this repo to **GitHub Pages** to play immediately, or open `index.html` locally to start blasting dragons with the power of proper spelling.

---

## 🎛️ How to Play (Rules of Engagement)

1. **Step into the Arena**: As soon as the game loads, a legendary monster will stare into your soul. 
2. **Read the Incantation**: Look at the glowing golden text beneath the boss's health bar. This is your spell.
3. **Type for Your Life**: Type that exact word into the input box. If you make a typo, your spell fizzes out and the dragon laughs at you.
4. **Beat the Clock**: If you take too long to type a word, the boss will smack you for 1 HP. You only have 5 HP. 
5. **Survive the Scaling**: Defeating a boss heals you slightly, but the next boss will have more HP and significantly less patience.
